About the Mall

Phoenix Palladium, India’s first premium luxury retail and entertainment at Lower Parel, Mumbai, presents an environment that spells refined elegance, while shoppers soak in the retail offerings, restaurants, cafes, spas, and more!

Phoenix Palladium houses over 250+ premium brands sprawled across millions of square feet. These include international brands like GUCCI, BURBERRY, BOTTEGA VENETA, TOD’S, JIMMY CHOO, MICHAEL KORS, COACH, MASSIMO DUTTI, CANALI, ARMANI EXCHANGE, PAUL SMITH, HUGO BOSS, FRANCK MULLER, HUBLOT, PANERAI & many more.

What makes Phoenix Palladium so irresistible is the exceptional shopping in an atmosphere of sophistication and refinement under one elegant roof. Its unique design expression makes it a striking structure in the architecture of the city that never sleeps.

Phoenix Palladium has become a landmark already. The retail and lifestyle mix comes alive with the luxury labels, high fashion brands, cafes, fine dining restaurants, spas, and entertainment.

This truly one-of-its-kind luxury retail space in India also provides for special services such as a Concierge Desk, Wi-Fi and Valet Parking for visitors.
